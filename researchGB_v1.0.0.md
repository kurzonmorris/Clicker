# Project Arasmas — Research Tree: Great Britain

> **Purpose:** Complete research tree for the Great Britain civilization. Every research item includes: era, cost, prerequisites, effects, and historical description.
> **Last Updated:** 2026-04-03
> **Status:** Template created — to be populated in a separate dedicated chat session
> **Related File:** `toolsandequipmentGB.md` (tools/equipment unlocked by this research)

---

## How to Use This File

- Each research item is defined with all data needed for the game's `data/settings/civilizations/great_britain/research.json` file
- Research items are organised by era
- Prerequisites reference other research items by their ID
- Historical descriptions are Great Britain-specific — other civilizations will have different text for the same/similar research
- Forward research items (researchable in the previous era) are marked with `[FORWARD]`

---

## Research Item Template

```
### [ID] Research Name
- **Era:** Which era this research belongs to
- **Era Available:** Earliest era this can be researched (may be one era before its own era if forward research)
- **Research Point Cost:** How many Research Points to complete
- **Coin Cost:** Coins to unlock after research is complete
- **Prerequisites:** Other research IDs that must be completed first
- **Effects:** What this research unlocks or improves
- **Unlocks:** Equipment, buildings, or capabilities unlocked
- **Historical Description (Great Britain):** The civilization-specific historical text shown to the player
```
